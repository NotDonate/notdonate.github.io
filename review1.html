Review By Cam' Ron Page

Brewdog Waterloo

★★★★★

Review by Cam' Ron Page: This pub based inside of one of London's biggest stations, Waterloo Station, is an amazing place to visit and I would highly recommend trying it out! This is pub has fantastic drinks and even food at an affordable price, seating arrangements that are convenient and allows for bigger parties to sit together to enjoy themselves, the pub even hosts social events such as table tennis or gaming sessions every fortnight. But the best thing about it? They have a giant slide that lets you quickly traverse from the upper floor to the ground floor! In case of that taking the stairs is too "generic" of a way to get around. So what are you waiting for! Next time you go out for a drink, come here instead, you'll have a great time!
