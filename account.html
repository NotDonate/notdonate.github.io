Hyde Park

★★★★

Review by Dontae: This park is awesome! It provides so much greenery and is a nice place to walk around. Though the best part... It's right in central london, meaning the are several ways to get here, bus, tube, you name it. Plus it also means that I'm easily able to visit other parts of the city before or after my trip to the park. Though my one complaint, is that it can get extremely busy, even in the winter months where its cold. Despite this I'd still highly recommend taking a trip around here if you get the time to.
